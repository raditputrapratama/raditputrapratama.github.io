About Me

Radit Putra Pratama, 22 August 2006. Lives in Kediri, East Java. a student who is currently studying at the Pawyatan Daha 1 Vocational School, Kediri City, majoring in multimedia.

Became interested in and built a career as a photographer & videographer since starting an internship at Pawon Cinema. So far he has created several works and has won various competitions.
